THE UPPER PRIMARY (CLASSES 4 to 8 inclusive)

Upper primary section covers classâ€™s 4-8 learners ages range between 10-13 years. It comprises of well-structured classrooms each with two streams mainly East and West and capacity of 25 to 30 learners per class. Major subjects taught are six as per syllabus of Ministry of Education, handled specific teachers, who are well equipped with the right knowledge on the subject in question. In addition, French and computer languages are taught in these classes .The core value is to impart knowledge to the learners in the right manner so as to make them prepare for future life. Apart from academics, we engage learners in co-curricular activities that enable the teachers to identify their hidden potentials or talents, where we nurture. Talents includes creative Art, debate, music, drama and sports among others.
