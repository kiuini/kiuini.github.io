THE LOWER PRIMARY (CLASSES 1 to 4 inclusive)

he lower primary section has learners of ages of between 6-9 years. It has six classrooms and each classroom has two streams mainly East & West .A classroom has seating capacity of 25-30 learners. The classes are handled by individual subject teachers and some are selected to act class head teachers. The teachers teach all the required subjects as per syllabus approved by Ministry of Education, other than French and Computers language which are taught with their respective teachers. From 2pm on Mondays to Thursdays, we engage our learners in co-curriculum activities such as storytelling, music, arts, clubs, swimming and library lessons. On Fridays, the learners are fully involved in sports activities: athletics, football etc. Our mission as lower primary is to provide at all times to all learners equal opportunities to develop academically, socially and spiritually and at their own pace.

Tr Prisca Bwire
