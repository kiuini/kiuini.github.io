Fee Payment

Day Scholar

A student can opt to be a day scholar or a boarder, most of the students (over 80% of the student population) are day scholars that come to school in the morning and return home in the afternoon.

Reporting time in the morning is 7:30 am and returning home time starts from 3:30 pm.

Boarders

The school has boarding facilities for both boys and girls that opted to be boarders. Those that are in upper primary, especially in class eight (8) are encourage to be boarders so as to get more coaching from the teacher in the evening.

The boarding facilities have a total capacity of hundred (100 ) boarders. The boarding charges are available for those interested in being boarders.

There is a matron and patron that take car of the girls and boys boarders respectively.
