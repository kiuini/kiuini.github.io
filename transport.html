TRANSPORT FACILITIES AND ZONAL AREA COVERAGE

Transport Facilities

A parent can opt to use School or private transport.

The school has a fleet of five (5) buses and a van which provide adequate transport coverage. The van is used during emergency cases such as when a bus has broken down.

There is a comprehensive insurance coverage for the fleet that also includes injury / death coverage for passengers in case emanating from an accident.

Charges for transport usage are available from the school administration, there are separate charges for one way or both ways.

Drivers, at end of year, go for medical and physical check ups including eye sight test. Only those that pass these check ups and tests are engaged by the school.

The school also ensures the drivers have all legal documents and permits that allow them to drive public service vehicles (P.S.V).

Being public service vehicles (P.S.V) each of these vehicles has an installed speed governor that allows a maximum speed of 80 Kilometers Per Hour (Kph).

The vehicles, at the end of each 12 months, are mechanically inspected and tested by the relevant government authority and provided with a certificate of road worthyness.

Zonal Area Coverage

For a smooth, efficient and effective transport logistical arrengement, the school management has demarcated catchment areas the transport fleet covers. Currently there are 5 zones areas, namely ;

Outside the earmarked zonal areas, the school encourages parents to use their own means or we link them to a private transporter that has been identified by the school.
